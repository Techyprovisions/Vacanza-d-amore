Don’t bring gifts,
bring love!

 

We feel very privileged to be able to celebrate our wedding in Tuscany with so many friends and family. Instead of traditional weddings gifts, we’d like to encourage you to make a donation to one of the following causes that are very dear to us:

  1. The Ocean Cleanup

  2. himate!
